Problems I Solve

If any of this feels familiar, we should talk.

Most clients don't come to me asking for "an Odoo module" or "an n8n workflow". They come because something in their operations is broken. Here's what that usually looks like.

🤖

Manual Workflows

Your team repeats the same tasks every day across spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected tools.

I build AI agents and n8n workflows that take that work off their plate — lead handling, ticketing, follow-ups, reporting.

n8n · Vertex AI · WhatsApp API
🧩

Scattered Tools

Your business is spread across WhatsApp, Sheets, Notion, and memory — nothing connects, nothing is the source of truth.

I consolidate it into one Odoo or microservice system with the dashboards, role-based access, and reports you can actually trust.

Odoo · Python · PostgreSQL
⚠️

Team Dependency

Everything breaks when one person is unavailable because the process lives in their head, not in a system.

I document the workflow, build the internal tools, and turn tribal knowledge into something the team can actually use without you.

Internal tools · Admin panels · SOPs
🎓

Scattered Learning

Your education content, student progress, and assessments are scattered without a structured system.

I build learning platforms with certificates, tracking, and AI-assisted content — not another generic LMS.

SvelteKit · AI Pipelines · Drizzle

Diagnose the Mess

  • Find repetitive tasks and manual bottlenecks
  • Spot scattered data and duplicated effort
  • Listen to the team that lives the workflow
  • Identify what's actually slowing growth
